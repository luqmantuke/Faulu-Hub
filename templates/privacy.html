Privacy Policy

Introduction

﻿

Faulu Hub is committed to protecting your personal information when you are using our services. We want our services to be safe and enjoyable environments for our users. This Privacy Policy relates to our use of any personal information you provide to us via online registration, content usage, by email, in letters or correspondence. It also relates to our use of any personal information you provide to us online or on mobile. From time to time, we will need to collect information about you. This Privacy Policy explains the following:

    what information Faulu Hub may collect about you;

  • how Faulu Hub will use information we collect about you;

Faulu Hub is committed to safeguarding your personal information. Whenever you provide such information, we pledge to be legally obliged with data protection legal provisions available locally and internationally. We do not accept any responsibility or liability for the privacy practices of such third party websites and your use of such websites is at your own risk. Information about you

﻿When you participate in, access or sign up to any of Faulu Hub's services, activities or online content, such as the API, the web application, discussion forum, newsletters, competitions, live chats, notice boards, emails, social networks or create an account using our online registration system we may receive personal information about you. This can consist of information such as your name, email address, mobile number, date of birth, educational institution/school, level of education or gender depending on the activity. By submitting your details, you enable Faulu Hub (and where applicable its contractors) to provide you with the services, activities or online content you select. Please note that sometimes we will require you to provide additional personal information for purposes of research. When we do this we will provide further information as to why we are collecting your information and how we will use it. Faulu Hub collect IP addresses from visitors of the web application. We also remotely track their usage of the content for purposes of analytics collection. This information is quantitative and may not necessarily contain private (qualitative) information.

How we will use your personal information

    Faulu Hub will use your personal information;

  • To provide our services and online content and to deal with your enquiries;
  • For "service administration purposes", which means that Faulu Hub may contact you for reasons related to the service or online content you have signed up for.

  • To provide you with information about our services, activities or online content;

Sharing your personal information

﻿

We will keep your information confidential except where disclosure is required or permitted by law, to government bodies and law enforcement agencies. Generally, we will only use your information within Faulu Hub. However, sometimes Faulu Hub engages third parties to process your information on our behalf. We require these third parties to comply strictly with its instructions and not to use your personal information for their own business purposes, unless you have explicitly consented to the use of your personal information in this way.

Offensive or inappropriate content

﻿

If you post or send offensive, inappropriate or objectionable content anywhere on Faulu Hub, we may use your personal information to stop such behaviour. Where Faulu Hub reasonably believes that you are or may be in breach of any applicable laws (e.g. because content you have posted may be defamatory), we may use your personal information to inform relevant third parties such as your school about the content and your behaviour.
